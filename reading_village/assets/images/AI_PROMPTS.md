## App Icon (1024x1024px)

```
A cohesive kawaii app icon for a reading village game. A photo-view scene with:

- The cat villager (same design: chubby, round body, short legs, small paws, simple dot eyes, tiny mouth) sitting under a tree from the park (same design as park.png)
- The cat villager is positioned on the right side of the tree, sitting and reading a cute book
- Camera angle: taken from the left side, focusing solely on this scenario (no other park elements)
- Background: kawaii-style garden with little pastel-colored flowers at the bottom (fully visible, not cut off), soft blue sky with fluffy white clouds above
- The ground should be a continuous grassy area, not a floating rectangle
- Color palette: soft pastels (pink, mint green, lavender, cream, light blue)
- Smooth gradients and subtle shadows for depth
- Kawaii style with simple, clean outlines
- No text or extra elements - everything flows together
- The image should have naturally rounded corners (like a standard Android app icon) - not a square with a round border
- Cozy, inviting atmosphere that emphasizes reading and relaxation

2D flat design with slight depth, 1024x1024px.
```
